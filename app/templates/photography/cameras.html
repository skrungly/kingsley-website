cameras

many of my best photos are taken with my dedicated digital cameras but i often have the most fun when using more restrictive cameras, like the one on my nintendo DSi. i'd also like to get my hands on a 35mm film camera so that i can try a more thoughtful approach to photography!

almost all of the photos in my gallery were taken with one of these cameras:

nikon D5100

this is my camera of choice when photography is the primary focus for a day out. i have three lenses which cover a good range of capabilities:

  • nikkor 18-55mm f/3.5-5.6G ED II (kit lens)
  • sigma 70-300mm f4-5.6 DG macro (equivalent to 105-450mm)
  • walimex 500mm f/8.0 (equivalent to 750mm)

canon ixus 185

a lot of my best photos have been taken on this tiny camera because i always keep it in my pocket. its focal range is decent, equivalent to 28‑224mm on a 35mm sensor like the one above, but it has fairly limited manual controls. being able to focus as near as 1 cm on a 20 MP sensor gives rise to some highly detailed macro shots!

nintendo DSi

the nintendo DSi has a very simplistic point-and-shoot camera system with a 0.3 MP sensor behind it, producing photos with a unique digital aesthetic. it has a decent selection of effects but, unsurprisingly, very few of them are useful for taking actual photos. my main DSi has a modified enclosure which i made by swapping parts between two separate black and white consoles.
